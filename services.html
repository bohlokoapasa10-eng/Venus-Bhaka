Plan Features

Gold

The Gold plan is a flexible plan , allowing a customer to choose any or all of the seven baked goods on the menu. This plan enables a customer to get the baked goods combo of their choice twice a week for the whole month. All these goods come in different flavours and allows a customer to customize a recipe for the goods they want or need. This plan has a specialized loyalty card to people who subscribe to it , allowing a customer to get discounts.

Silver

The Silver plan is standard , only allowing customers to choose from five baked goods from the menu. It is a twice a month plan , still alowing customers to choose flavors of their goods.

Bronze

The Bronze plan is the basic plan that only allows customers to choose three baked goods from the menu . It is a once a month plan , still having the benefit of choosing flavours of goods.
